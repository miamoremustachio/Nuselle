# *LINUX*
## 1991, Community contributors

üêß A family of open-source Unix-like operating systems based on Linux [[Kernel|kernel]]

- Was designed by [Linus Torvalds](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Linus_Torvalds "Linus Torvalds")
