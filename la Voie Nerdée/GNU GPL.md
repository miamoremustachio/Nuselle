The **GNU General Public Licenses**, or simply **GPL** are a series of widely used free software licenses that guarantee end users the freedom to run, share, or modify the software.

The GPL was the first [copyleft](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Copyleft) license available for general use. 
It was originally written by [Richard Stallman](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Richard_Stallman "Richard Stallman"), the founder of the [[GNU|GNU Project]].
